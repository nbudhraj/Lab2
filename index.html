My Last 3 Days

Day 1: "Monday Motivation"

April 5, 2021

Like most people, I often dread Mondays, but this Monday was a pleasant surprise, as I ended up having a somewhat productive day and actually enjoyed going to my lectures. On Mondays I typically watch my MMW lectures and I also attended my weekly MUS 17 lectures/discussions. Both classes offerered interesting insights on modern artforms, helping me contextualize the societal implications of art pieces. Today was also a special Monday because the NCAA Men's Basketball Championship was that evening. Although the game was not as entertaining as some of he past tournament games, it was still pretty fun to watch Baylor University dominate Gonzaga University, who were heavily favored to win the game. Overall, the inspiring stories about Hip-Hop and art I learned during my MUS 17 and MMW 15, along with the adversities the Baylor basketball team overcame to win the NCAA tournament, provided me with motivation to be on top of my homework for the rest of the week.


Highlights of the Day

  • My MUS17 discussion, where my classmates and I discussed our personal relationship to Hip-Hop

    At the beginning our section, the TA played us an interesting and deeply personal song by rapper Jean Grae

  • Learning about Realism, Impressionism, Cubism, and Dadaism in modern art through my MMW15 lectures

    In class, we learned about the significance of Salvador Dali's famous painting, The Persistance of Memory


  • Watching Baylor upset Gonzaga in the NCAA National Championships


Lowlights of the Day

  1. Didn't sleep too well the night before, so was a bit fatigued throughout the day
  2. Finding out that I have a CSE 101 Quiz on Friday
  3. Falling behind on some homework
